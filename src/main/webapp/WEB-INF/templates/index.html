Race Management System


 About us...

Project Cadence is a software component designed to assist coaches, sponsors etc. during cycle races to analyze the live tracking information and make decisions. This software solution will provide the ability to evaluate the team’s statistics live on a dashboard, understand positions, and make better decisions. It will enable the coaches to stay up-to- date with all the problems during the race. This will reduce the time for information relay between the riders and the coach. The strategy can be re-evaluated quickly and decisions to save the game can be made. The real time flow of data from the mobile application to the web dashboard will provide information on cyclist’s position throughout the race. The communication channel will enable timely exchange of messages especially in races where voice communication is not allowed. The single and multi-messaging capability will prove beneficial for sending specific messages to team member(s).
